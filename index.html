Intresting Facts About Teddy Bear

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903.

Intresting Facts About Flying Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors

Intresting Facts About Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
  • An adult Panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Intresting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • They are one of only a small number of species to pass the 'mirror test' – a test of self recognition.
  • They can also recognise each letter of the human alphabet, differentiate between photographs

Intresting Facts About Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
